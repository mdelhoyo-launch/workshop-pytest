Youtube search.
We are going to automate a youtube search while appyling all the explained subjects.

1. First start creating the directory:

youtube_search
│
├── pages/
│   ├── __init__.py
│   ├── youtube_home_page.py
│   └── youtube_search_results_page.py
├── tests/

│   └── test_youtube_search.py
├── requirements.txt

2. Create the "requirements.txt" file, which will contain all our needed modules.

3. Create empty __init__.py files in the pages and tests directories (to make the directories accessible for pytest).
